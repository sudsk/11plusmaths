DISTANCE, SPEED & TIME (Questions 1-10)
1. Medium
Mrs. Brown travelled 30 km at 40 km/hr, then 45 km at 60 km/hr. What was her average speed?
2. Easy
A car travels 240 km in 3 hours. What is its average speed in km/hr?
3. Medium
James walks 8 km to the shops at 4 km/hr, then takes a bus home at 32 km/hr. What is his average speed for the entire journey?
4. Hard
A cyclist travels 45 km in 2 hours 30 minutes, then rests for 30 minutes, then travels another 30 km in 2 hours. What is his average speed for the entire journey including rest time?
5. Medium
Two trains start from opposite ends of a 300 km track at the same time. One travels at 80 km/hr and the other at 70 km/hr. After how long will they meet?
6. Easy
Lucy runs 12 km in 1 hour 20 minutes. What is her average speed in km/hr?
7. Hard
A delivery van travels 120 km at 60 km/hr, then gets stuck in traffic and travels the next 40 km at 20 km/hr. What is the average speed for the entire journey?
8. Medium
Tom walks to school at 5 km/hr taking 24 minutes. He cycles back home at 15 km/hr. How long does his return journey take?
9. Hard
A plane flies 800 km with a tailwind of 50 km/hr in 2 hours. What is the plane's speed in still air?
10. Medium
Sarah drives 180 km at 90 km/hr, then 120 km at 80 km/hr. What is her average speed for the complete journey?

19. Medium

20. Easy
