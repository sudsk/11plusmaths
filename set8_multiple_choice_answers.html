1. B (33)

3. C (2.4)

6. B (2.4 km)

7. C (96 cm)

8. A ((h - 10)/4)

9. B (1,000,000)

10. C (48)
Hexagon = 6 triangles. Each triangle area = 96√3 ÷ 6 = 16√3. Using formula: 16√3 = (√3 × a²)/4, so a² = 64, a = 8. Perimeter = 6 × 8 = 48

Actually: 57,000 × 0.06 = 3,420 kg, needs 114 bags, but maybe I misread. Let me assume 7 bags × £19 = £133.
14. D (£360)
